Brand Story

In the digital age of education, lesson planning is not just about taking notes on lecture content, but also requires flexibility, creativity and time saving. However, many teachers still have difficulty entering mathematical formulas, standardizing lesson plans according to regulations, and managing documents effectively.

Edu Smart was born to solve these problems, providing a smart lesson planning platform, helping teachers save time, optimize the teaching process and easily collaborate with colleagues. With Edu Smart, teachers no longer have to worry about time-consuming manual lesson planning. Let technology help you focus on what matters most - imparting knowledge effectively!

Welcome To EduSmart

In an increasingly innovative education world, teachers still face a huge workload: lesson planning, approving teaching plans, updating documents... That leaves them with less time to focus on the most important thing - inspiring students. EduSmart (ES) was born to change that. We believe that technology not only supports but also helps teachers to break through, optimize time and improve teaching quality and workflow. With specialized AI for middle and high school curriculum, ES helps teachers create lesson plans quickly, intelligently and flexibly. The online approval feature helps shorten the workflow of tasks. ES will be a companion of teachers on the journey of educational innovation.
