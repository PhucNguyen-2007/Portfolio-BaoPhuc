Brand Story

BEAST is one of the leading car tuning brands in Vietnam, famous for its luxury and high-quality cars. Established in 1999, this brand has gone through many ups and downs but still maintains its prestige and reputation. In addition, BEAST also stands out for its innovations in technology, especially in the areas of safety and performance, helping it maintain a competitive position in the global auto industry.

With the name BEAST, we want to emphasize our ability to manufacture and upgrade vehicles with high-capacity engines. The cars that pass through our hands will become stronger, sturdier and more personalized.

Our competitors include car showrooms with car tuning and other car tuning companies in Vietnam. These are all potential competitors that can attract our customers. Vision: To become the leading car tuning company in Southeast Asia, bringing unique, high-quality experiences to customers who are passionate about cars. Mission: Not only to inspire passion but also to bring customers unique and emotional experiences. In addition, to build a close-knit community where people can share their passion together.

Brand Indentify Design

The BEAST logo draws inspiration from the dynamic and powerful design of an F1 racing car's steering wheel, symbolizing the high-performance and cutting-edge quality of BEAST's products. At its core, the logo features two stylized, mirrored "B" letters, representing not only the brand name but also key attributes such as Best, Brilliant, and Beauty. The vibrant orange-red hue (#EF3924) dominates the design, evoking energy, passion, and an unwavering commitment to excellence. This color choice reflects BEAST's dedication to exceeding customer expectations and delivering solutions that are bold, dynamic, and full of enthusiasm.
